## Overview

Home Academy is a comprehensive online learning platform that brings education to your home. It provides interactive courses, progress tracking, and collaboration tools for students and teachers.

## Key Features

- **Course Library**: Access to various educational courses and materials
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor learning progress and achievements
- **Interactive Lessons**: Engaging content with multimedia support
- **User Dashboard**: Personalized learning experience
- **Responsive Interface**: Works seamlessly on all devices

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: React.js, CSS3
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js
- **Database**: MongoDB, Mongoose
- **Authentication**: JWT
- **Deployment**: Vercel

## Results

Home Academy successfully provides an accessible and engaging learning environment with intuitive navigation and comprehensive course management features.
