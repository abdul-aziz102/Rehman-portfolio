## Overview

A modern and professional company portfolio website designed to showcase business services, completed projects, and team expertise. Built with attention to detail and user experience.

## Key Features

- **Service Showcase**: Detailed presentation of company services
- **Project Gallery**: Portfolio of completed projects with descriptions
- **Team Section**: Meet the team with profiles and expertise
- **Contact Form**: Easy communication with potential clients
- **Smooth Animations**: Enhanced user experience with modern transitions

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: React.js, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
- **Deployment**: Vercel

## Results

The portfolio website effectively presents the company's capabilities with a professional design, smooth navigation, and responsive layout that works perfectly across all devices.
